ABOUT ME!

I am a B.Tech graduate, and I consider myself a strong-minded and creative individual, always prepared to take on challenges. My dedication lies in applying my skills, constantly seeking opportunities to learn from experienced experts.

In terms of education, I pursued my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at GITAM School of Technology in Doddaballapura, Bengaluru, with a commendable GPA of 9.10.

I gained valuable experience as a Project Trainee at the Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, DRDO, from June 2022 to August 2022. I worked on Fake News Detection under the guidance of Scientist "F" Siddartha Banerjee, utilizing various classifiers and transformers to enhance accuracy.

My skill set includes proficiency in Python, C, data structures, algorithms, HTML, JavaScript, MySQL, CSS, and Database Management System. I also have a keen interest in Machine Learning and possess foundational knowledge in Artificial Intelligence.

I've undertaken several personal projects, such as developing a robot with obstacle avoidance and path-following capabilities using Arduino UNO (October 2019), creating an image detection system with Raspberry Pi and Tensorflow (May 2021), and working on a Machine Learning project for Fake News Detection (June 2022 - August 2022). Additionally, I embarked on an Emotion Detection project in video and audio using Keras and Convolutional Neural Networks, extending it to classify genders in various audio files (September 2022 - April 2023).

I was selected for a NAAC review from GITAM Bengaluru, showcasing my academic excellence. Furthermore, I actively engaged in club activities, serving as an Executive Team Member for The Speaking Club at GITAM Bengaluru and later as a Social Media Manager for Github GITAM Bengaluru.
